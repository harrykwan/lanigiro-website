Lanigiro is a mobile-based NFT social media platform, powered by Augmented Reality technology. Users can buy digital collectibles, exhibit them at will and socialise with the mass society. The platform aims to enhance interactive usage of NFTs and connect the mass society closer to creators and their owned digital assets.

Product

an NFT metaverse powered by AR technology. by using “token2Obj” service, users can convert nFT contents into 3D objects. Users will then be able to exhibit, show off and communicate through these 3D nFTs anywhere anytime, enhancing the interactive-ness of the creations.
